Voicemail Card 13

Voicemail Template - The 4-Step Formula

The 4 Steps
1

Name

State your name and dealership

2

Reason

One sentence why you're calling

3

Call to Action

Tell them what to do next

4

Stop

Don't ramble. End the message.

Example

"Hi [Name], Tyler at Garber Chevrolet Midland. Calling about your vehicle inquiry. When you get a moment, give me a call at [number]. I'll keep it quick."

Rule: Keep every voicemail under 20 seconds. If you have to think about what to say next - hang up.
