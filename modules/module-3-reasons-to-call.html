Module 3

Strategic Reasons to Call

Never run out of reasons to call. These are legitimate, value-driven reasons to contact customers - each with a complete script ready to use.

The Problem

Most salespeople stop calling because they feel like they don't have a reason. "I don't want to bother them." The truth is: you're not bothering anyone when you bring real value. Four call types. Four real reasons to pick up the phone. Pick the one that fits, use the script, and set the appointment.

1

Inventory Call

"Hey [Name], this is Tyler over at Garber Chevrolet Midland. I wanted to reach out because we just took in something similar to what you were looking for and I wanted to let you know before it gets advertised heavily. Do you have a couple of minutes so I can tell you about it?"

WHEN TO USE:

When new trade-ins arrive or fresh inventory matches a customer's previous interest. Check your CRM for customers who looked at similar vehicles - this call feels personal and timely.

2

Equity Call

"Hey [Name], this is Tyler at Garber Chevrolet Midland. I'm reaching out because market values have shifted recently and your vehicle may have some positive equity right now. If we could look at upgrading you into something newer at a similar monthly payment, would that be worth reviewing together?"

WHEN TO USE:

When market values increase, typically during spring/summer or when interest rates change. Also great for customers 18-24 months into their loan - their equity position may have improved significantly.

3

Service Lane Call

"Hey [Name], this is Tyler in sales. I see your vehicle is here for service today. While it's being taken care of, I can quickly run some upgrade numbers for you - just so you're aware of your current options. No pressure at all, just information. Would you have a few minutes while you wait?"

WHEN TO USE:

When a customer's vehicle is in for service - this is a warm handoff opportunity. The customer is already on-site and in a car-related mindset. Coordinate with your service team daily to identify these opportunities.

4

Anniversary Call

"Hey [Name], this is Tyler from Garber Chevrolet Midland. I just wanted to personally thank you again for your business and make sure everything is still going great with your [Vehicle]. Is there anything at all I can help you with?"

WHEN TO USE:

At 30, 60, and 90 days after purchase, and again at the 1-year anniversary. These calls build loyalty, generate referrals, and keep you top-of-mind when they (or someone they know) are ready to buy again.

Note: rotate your reasons between calls so each one feels fresh. Keep notes in your CRM about which reason you used last. The goal is always the same: set the appointment.
