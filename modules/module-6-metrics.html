Module Objective

Turn your sales activity into a math equation. Know exactly how many calls you need to make to hit your income target - every single month.

The Income Funnel

Every sale starts at the top. The numbers narrow down at each stage. Your job is to fill the top so the bottom produces results.

What to Track

Track these five numbers every single day. If you don't track it, you can't improve it.

1
Calls Made

Every dial counts. Track outbound attempts, even if no one picks up.

2
Conversations

How many calls turned into actual two-way conversations.

3
Appointments Set

Firm commitments with a date and time. Not "maybe later."

4
Show Rate

What percentage of your set appointments actually walked in the door.

5
Close Rate

Of those who showed, how many bought. This is your craft - sharpen it.

Reverse Engineer Your Income

Start with the end. How much do you want to make? Then work backwards through the funnel.

1.

Monthly income goal - Pick a real number. Write it down.

2.

Average commission per deal - What do you actually earn per unit?

3.

Units needed - Income goal divided by average commission.

4.

Appointments needed - Use your show rate and close rate to calculate.

5.

Daily calls - Divide total conversations by 22 working days. That's your daily number.
